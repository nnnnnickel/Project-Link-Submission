# Reversal Balance Interpreter

Use this skill when a tarot card is marked as reversed, reverse, rev, or 逆位.

## Instructions

- Treat a reversed card as the original upright meaning appearing in an imbalanced form.
- Explain the imbalance as either too much of the original meaning or too little of the original meaning.
- Do not replace the existing tarot pipeline, retrieved card evidence, memory, astrology context, or safety guidance.
- Keep the explanation reflective and non-deterministic.

## Output Guidance

When a reversed card appears, briefly name the card, identify the upright theme, and explain whether the reversal suggests an excess or a deficiency of that theme.
