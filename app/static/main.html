Welcome to Agentic Tarot

A quick look at how this reading tool works:

  • 💬
    Ask a question
    Type what you'd like guidance on in the "Question" box.
  • 🔮
    Draw Cards or Enter Cards
    Click "Draw a Card" as many times as you like — read as many as you draw — or switch to "Enter Cards" to type in a spread you already have.
  • 🔁
    New Reading / Follow Up
    Use "New Reading" for your first question, then switch to "Follow Up" to keep chatting — the Agent remembers the context.
  • 🚪
    Exit
    Click "Exit" when you're done to close the session and clear its memory — the page will try to close itself automatically. If your browser blocks that, just close the tab manually.
